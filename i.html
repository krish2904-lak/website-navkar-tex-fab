Technical Information and Documentation

Fabric Care Guides

Cotton: Machine wash cold, tumble dry on low, iron on medium heat.

Silk: Dry clean or hand wash in cold water, lay flat to dry, avoid direct sunlight.

Polyester: Machine wash warm, tumble dry low, use fabric softener to reduce static.

Fabric Specifications

Cotton: Composition - 100% cotton, Durability - High, Weight - 200 GSM, Care - Machine wash cold.

Silk: Composition - 100% silk, Durability - Moderate, Weight - 100 GSM, Care - Hand wash or dry clean.

Polyester: Composition - 100% polyester, Durability - Very high, Weight - 150 GSM, Care - Machine wash warm.

Certification and Compliance Information

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100: Ensures no harmful substances are used in the production process.

Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS): Certified organic fibers, free from pesticides and toxic dyes.

ISO 9001 Certification: Quality management standard ensuring reliable, high-quality products.
